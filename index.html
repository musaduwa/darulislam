About Darul Islam - Islamic Center

Our Islamic Learning Centre is a renowned institution dedicated to imparting Islamic knowledge and values to individuals of all ages. Located in the heart of GRA Daura, Katsina State, Nigeria, our center serves as a hub for spiritual growth, intellectual development, and community engagement.

Our mission is to provide a comprehensive Islamic education that fosters spiritual, intellectual, and personal growth. We aim to create a community of knowledgeable, compassionate, and responsible individuals who embody the values of Islam.

Offered Services

Quran Learning

Offers opportunities for individuals to learn and recite the Quran with proper Tajweed, promoting spiritual growth and understanding of Islamic teachings.

Islamic Lecture

Provides education on Islamic principles, values, and practices, fostering moral and ethical development in individuals and communities.

Mosque Development

Focuses on constructing and maintaining mosques as centers of worship, education, and community gathering, enhancing communal harmony and spiritual connection.

Helps Poor

Implements initiatives to support the underprivileged by providing financial aid, food, shelter, and other necessities, embodying the Islamic principle of compassion and charity.
